Features

🎯

80 Levels

Progress through carefully crafted levels from Easy to Expert difficulty. Each level presents a unique challenge.

⭐

Star Ratings

Earn 1-3 stars based on your score. Perfect your strategy to achieve 3 stars on every level.

🔤

Word Discovery

Tap adjacent letters horizontally, vertically, or diagonally to form words from our dictionary of 2000+ words.

💡

Power-ups

Use hints or double your points when you need that extra edge to complete a level.

🏆

Game Center

Compete on leaderboards and unlock achievements. See how you rank against players worldwide.

📱

Play Offline

No internet required. Play anywhere, anytime with full offline support.

How to Play

1

Select Letters

Tap on adjacent letters to form a word. Letters can connect horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.

2

Build Words

Create words of 3 or more letters. Longer words earn bonus multipliers and more points.

3

Reach the Target

Score enough points to complete the level and earn stars. Aim for 3 stars on every level!
